于长洪（Yu Changhong）

I am currently taking a gap year to explore my research interests and advance my ideas. My work is dedicated to understanding the structure of society, focusing on two main aspects: narrative structure dynamics and the interplay of social dimensions. My exploration is embodied in two key projects: "Structure of Narratives" and "Agent Based Evolutionary Dynamics: 1 population 2 payoffs 3 strategies (ABED-123)."

"Structure of Narratives" employs computational text analysis to map out the similarity of narrative structures and evolutionary trajectories of large-scale texts. "ABED-123" models the social landscape that emerges from agents operating under the influence of multidimensional payoffs. These efforts aim to understand the complex mechanisms that underpin our social world.
